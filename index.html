Ultimate platform for staked assets

Supernova will enable liquid staking on Cosmos througha combination of ICA, proprietary module and IBC query.

How it works

01

Liquid Staking

  • Stake and mint your snAssets to unlock your liquidity while staking!
  • snAssets also auto-compound your staking reward and provide the best yield
  • You can redeem your assets by burning snAssets
  • We shall bring more updates to add utility to the snAssets!

02

Staked Swap

  • Swap your shadow tokens through our novel AMM
  • The pricing of staked assets has been arbitrary until now on other protocols
  • Supernova’s staked swap will take various factors like unbonding period and interest rate to provide the best swap experience

03

Governance

  • Vote with NOVA and participate in governance on all app-chains through Supernova validator
  • NOVA will become the linchpin of the governance on Cosmos ecosystem
